Hi, I'm Holly!

After graduating with my Bachelor's Degree in Radio-Television-Film from The University of Texas, I realized that wanted to pursue a career in web and app development because although I love art and design, I love having certainty in my work. I have many aspirations within the intersection of technology and entertainment; primarily, I want to incorporate my design skills to innovate digital processes while understanding how users interact with media to improve their experiences. Currently, I'm honing my skills as a web developer at MIT's Coding Bootcamp and experimenting with web animation and JavaScript. I hope to continue developing streaming interfaces to facilitate the pairing of lesser-known videos with people who could fall in love with the messages they contain. Feel free to reach out!
